About

As Bakul set up libraries, it perceived that there were few attractive books in Odia for helping children to learn reading and to fall in love with books. And the statistics are shocking - (About 50% children in Grade V cannot read the Grade II textbook).

Bakul Books is an attempt to address that problem by creating books for early learners at par with the best in the world.
